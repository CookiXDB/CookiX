At dim=3 each object's sheaf stalk is a unit vector on the sphere, and each relation is a rotation of it. The orange vector is the anchor's meaning carried through every relation on the path. The gap between where it lands and the blue target stalk is the composition residual — 0 means a perfectly coherent reasoning chain. (Restriction maps are currently random placeholders, so expect large residuals: that is the unproven layer being shown honestly, not hidden.)
